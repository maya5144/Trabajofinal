Biography 

Hi! My name is Marialys Lugo. I'm 21 years old and I am a Puerto Rican artist.

My skills as a Graphic Designer are: creativity, mastery of color theory,art history and a greater command of programs such as: Illustrator and Photoshop.

As a graphic designer I like to capture in the work 2do, my point of view and that of others whetherit is a topic or an idea. I can create what you think is not real with programs that help you show what others do not see in your mind, I can give identify to a place with just a logo and I can create more things as a graphic designer when people understand the message that I want to convey. Being a graphic designer is not about making a pretty design and already, being a graphic designer is about give him design meaning.
